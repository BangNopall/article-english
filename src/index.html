Effectiveness of Using Ruang Guru as Learning Media on Student Understanding at Lessons

Ruang Guru, as an education technology platform in Indonesia, provides access to high-quality learning resources that support student interaction and engagement. With interactive features such as animated videos, quizzes, and learning materials that can be accessed at any time, Ruang Guru is designed to meet students' diverse learning needs. Our review results show that using Ruang Guru significantly improves students' understanding and interest in various subjects. Students using this platform show higher levels of engagement and better academic results compared to traditional learning methods. This success can be attributed to the personalized learning approach and the platform's ability to create a dynamic learning environment. Overall, Ruang Guru proved to be effective as a classroom learning method, positively contributing to student understanding and creating a more engaging and interactive learning experience.
